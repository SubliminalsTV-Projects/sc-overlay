Erkul Loadout URLs
Paste an erkul share URL per ship. The overlay reads the ship from each build automatically — when that ship spawns in-game, it switches to it. Click a row to make it the active overlay now.
Game Log + Auto-Switch
Click Detect to find your Star Citizen install and pick the channel you play (LIVE / PTU / EPTU / etc.). It auto-selects the one you played most recently; click another below to switch.
subliminal.gg Sync
Get a token at subliminal.gg/blueprints → “Connect the desktop tracker”. Received blueprints and the mission you're tracking sync to your account automatically — leave the field blank to keep the saved token.
Binding Chart Overlay
Pick a PNG (transparency supported — e.g. your joystick binding chart) and press the hotkey in-game to toggle it full-screen over gameplay. It's click-through, so it won't interfere. Changing the hotkey requires restarting the app.
Overlay Performance
Turn this off if the overlay crashes your game. Blueprint tracking and collection sync keep running without it — you just lose the on-screen panel. Takes effect immediately, no restart. Tip: the binding-chart hotkey (Ctrl+Alt+K) opens another overlay window, so leave that off too while testing.
Off by default. The overlay renders on the CPU, which avoids crashes some GPUs (especially AMD) hit when the overlay runs over the game. Turn this on only if you have GPU headroom and don't get crashes. Changing this requires restarting the app.
